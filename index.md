# URL Sharer

**The fastest way to share any webpage — to any platform.**

Click the URL Sharer icon in your toolbar and instantly share the current tab to 14 platforms:

- 💬 **Messaging** — WhatsApp, Telegram
- 📱 **Social** — Facebook, Twitter/X, Threads, Bluesky, Tumblr
- 💼 **Professional** — LinkedIn
- 🗳️ **Communities** — Reddit, Hacker News, Pinterest
- 📧 **Classic** — Email
- 🔗 **Utilities** — Copy to Clipboard, Generate QR Code

---

## Why thousands of people use URL Sharer

- ✅ No account or sign-in required
- ✅ Works on every webpage — articles, videos, products, docs
- ✅ One click — no copying, no tab-switching, no hassle
- ✅ Lightweight — no background tracking, no bloat
- ✅ 100% open source (GPL v3)

Perfect for bloggers, journalists, researchers, students, and anyone who shares links regularly.

---

## How it works

1. Browse to any page
2. Click the URL Sharer icon in your toolbar
3. Pick your platform — done ✓
