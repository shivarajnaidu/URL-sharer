Privacy Policy for URL Sharer

Last updated: March 24, 2026

URL Sharer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or 
browsing information. All operations are performed locally in your browser.

The extension accesses the current tab's URL solely to copy it to your clipboard 
upon your explicit action. This data never leaves your device.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.
